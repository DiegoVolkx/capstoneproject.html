My Hobbies

Very important habit in my life. You can have all the success you want, but if you don't have a good health, you'll never be able to enjoy it. I mostly focus on hypertrophy and strenth training by using calisthenics. The gym environment is a little too much for me; too much noise and waiting and there's not fresh air. On the other hand, training outdoors, right on the nature, feels amazing

Learning Languages

My goal is to know 10 languages fluently someday. I love the feeling of being able to talk a new language, especially if it's a language that's not so spoken.
